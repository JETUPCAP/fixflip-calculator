Fix & Flip Deal Analyzer — V1.0 Release Notes
Jet Up Capital | May 2026

Overview
V1.0 marks the official launch of the Jet Up Capital Fix & Flip Deal Analyzer — an interactive, web-based calculator designed to help real estate investors quickly assess deal viability, understand their loan structure, and submit deals directly to Jet Up Capital for a term sheet.

Features
Deal Calculator

Real-time loan structure analysis based on borrower tier and FICO score
Five borrower experience tiers (Tier 1–5) with corresponding loan parameters
Four renovation categories: Light, Moderate, Heavy, and Extensive
Currency-formatted inputs for purchase price, rehab budget, ARV, and taxes & insurance
Automatic LTC, ARLTV, and Total LTC calculations
12-month loan term displayed in loan structure

Interest & Fee Logic

Dual interest calculation methods:

Full Boat — interest on full loan amount for loans under $100,000
As Disbursed — initial loan charged full period, rehab holdback averaged for loans $100,000 and above


Origination fee: 1% lender (flat) + greater of 1% or $2,500 broker, displayed as a single line item

Eligibility Rules

Minimum loan amount: $50,000
Minimum down payment: $10,000
Tier and FICO-based eligibility matrix with clear ineligible messaging

Cost Breakdown

Estimated cash to close including down payment, origination, processing fees, interest reserve, title costs, and taxes & insurance
Full deal analysis with projected profit and cash-on-cash return
Pass/Fail exit test based on borrower tier requirements

Deal Submission Modal

"Submit My Deal" CTA pulls live calculator values into a deal summary
Google Maps Places Autocomplete for subject property address (US only)
HubSpot integration — submits contact info and all deal data directly to CRM
Auto-triggers HubSpot email notification to Jet Up Capital team
Success confirmation message upon submission

Design & Branding

Jet Up Capital branding with custom jet icon
Dark theme optimized for web and mobile
Embedded on jetupcapital.com via WordPress/Kubio


Known Limitations

Google Maps Autocomplete requires an active billing account on Google Cloud
Calculator data overwrites existing HubSpot contact properties on repeat submissions — Deal object migration planned for future version
Interest rate is hardcoded at 10.99% I/O — rate flexibility planned for V2
